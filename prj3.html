Medical Image Annotation Tool

Summary

C++, Qt based Medical image annotation tool. We support DICOM format medical images including CT, MRI, Ultrasounds. Users can view the volume by moving, and rotating the perspective of the 3D volume. User can annotate masks with manual annotation tools, also load the saved masks. Users can annotate masks with manual annotation tools, also load the saved masks.
